
- Serverless does not mean "no servers", it means we don't need to manage servers
- so anything that service that is managed can be referred to as serverless (no this is not true)

managed and serverless are separate concepts

managed means that someone else manages the operation of the service such as scaling, patching, provisioning, HA and failover. RDS is managed by AWS but they are always on service 

serverless on the other hand means that there is no server from the perspective of the user or the client interacting with the service
